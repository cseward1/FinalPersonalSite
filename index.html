About

a

Hi, I am Courtney Seward. A freelance web designer, front-end developer and digital planner based in Nashville,TN .

With a bachelor's in English and Psychology, my original goal after graduating from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville was to teach Literature. However, after college I realized this wasn't the vocational path I wished to continue exploring and moved back to Memphis the Fall of 2016 feeling more unsure than ever in what my next steps would be. It wasn’t until I attended a ‘Girl’s Who Code’ workshop on a whim this past Winter that I realized the opportunity and growth in the tech world. This workshop taught me and a handful of other beginners the very basics of code yet I became fascinated by all the lines of code that lie behind what is presented to us on our computer screens every day. I love that coding gives you the opportunity to be both creative, yet technical and challenges your ability to effectively problem solve. I chose to study and be an apprentice at Nashville Software School because of the challenge and great opportunity that being a Web Developer could contribute to my life through the education and assistance of this program.
